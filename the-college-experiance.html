The college experiance

Chamar boy | 01 Mar 2021 | Updated on 25 Apr 2021

You people have nothing to worry about, you’ll get jobs easily. The problem is for people like us

Ankur quipped while we are discussing the prospects of getting job after college.

We are discussing the prospects of getting job after college. The uncertainty caused by past IT recessions trigger this. Weather is pleasant. We are sitting outside a Nirulas’ under picnic umbrellas. This is part of a laid back market complex which does not do much business. It is walking distance from college, we love to take a long walk from college to here.

Ashish has been kicked out from the group, and some friends choose to stay with him. I wanted to, but I choose to stay where Anusha seems to be comfortable. This has reduced the number from around 15 to 5. We are bunking some class, sitting outside a food joint. I am holding Anusha’s hand, she might have felt my hand twitch.

Being a perennial back bencher and flunking in numerous examinations (total 5 papers till now), I do not know how to respond to this unexpected attack. Being a SC/ST student, it has become apparent to me that I cannot be just lazy, average or not interested, I am just an incompetent loser who does not deserve to sit between these enlightened beings. They seem to be doing me a favor by caring to listen to me.

After a long silence, considering the path of least resistance, i decided to change the topic.

The job market is difficult after the recession. All of us have some hard time ahead of us

But the Jack Maa look alike had to make make his point.

Why do you why people like you would look into the private job market? There’s so many of amazing opportunities available in the government sector. And believe me, you will earn far more than the private sector

Oh, somebody say something……silence!!

It’s not for everyone to see things your way.

I think that ended the discussion.

Oh, the crucial part of this open discrimination practiced by teachers, students and even chaprasis of this great institute – Bharati Vidyapeeth, Delhi and the even greater dumb-ass university it represents – Guru Gobind Singh Indraprastha University, was allocating the roll numbers – I think first 6 roll numbers represent the reserved community – SC/STs or chuda/chamar as thought of by the these enlightened and entitled middle class chutias.

I looked at Anusha as she is looking into my eyes. She is asking me to ignore this, but I cant seem to decide. We decide to go back to college. She has been one of those rare humans who has offered me parity – of thought, ability….respect of being treated like another human being.

We were happy gang of 15 middle class misfits, who who liked each other some of them had romantic inclinations. Some things have changed. Now we are 5/7 people (2 of them cant seem to decide).

Anusha is like a friend I never deserved or worked for. Of all the people in my life, I think she is the only one who feels what I am going through. I have never shared details of the legal and moral issues I am facing at home, but she only knows about Gunjan, and how I am still deeply in love with a with her.

I feel i am selfishly using her. I have no prospects, a loser to the core, still she prefers me. Even I don’t prefer myself these days. She still does.

Gunjan….I don’t know if she will be back or she has gone for good. I cant stop thinking about her. Maybe she is dating a stud she deserves…maybe she is being tricked by one of the thousand bastards who seem to be following her day and night. Anyways, I don’t seem to deserve her. She has a good idea or some suspicion of me being an undeserving chamar who tricked her into accepting my proposal…..good that nothing happened….this guilt of tricking her would have killed me.

Here is Anusha, the best of girls I would ever meet….holding my hand despite knowing my reality. I can feel love when she holds my hand.

And I don’t know how it happened. I don’t even remember the first time I held her hand, it seemed to have happened spontaneously.

As far as i can remember, she was talking me about a guy she seemed to have been dating, and it did not end well…i understood….and maybe that is how we became friends.

Much of the things between us seemed to be unsaid. She will wait for me, I would wait for her….find any simple excuse to be with her…..go on long walks….bunk classes to be with her. She did not seem to mind sitting on my old scooter.

She somehow understood….and I could feel her….her hands told me all i needed to know….some weird telepathy shit….or i am delusional.

I know what hurts her, I know when I hurt her, and how could I make it alright. She never hurt me…..and when she did….it was brutal

Coming back to the present moment,This awkward argument means Jack Maa is targeting me now. After kicking Ashish and his loyalists out, everyone in this leftover group of 7 is a bloody beta, all ugly, short or severely limited in intellect….basically an un-date-able set of severely undeserving poor specimen of the male form. No one Anusha or any conscious female or any functional girl would ever consider.

Or am I about to be proved wrong???
