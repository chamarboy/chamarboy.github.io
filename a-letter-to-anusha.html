A letter to Anusha

Chamar boy | 01 Sep 2020 | Updated on 18 Apr 2021

I loved you. Something in me still loves and misses you.

My long due closure.

Anusha, of all the females i have known in my life, you were the most kind, affectionate, intelligent and million other good things.

You were flawless.

I really wanted to be good for you….for the fact that i could not imagine not having your voice in my life.

As you had given hundreds of hours of me whining about my ex girlfriend Gunjan, I wanted to be sure.

For me, you were pure….an angel….maybe a god.

I really wanted to be sure, when i commit to you I commit for good, not as a insecure rebound from Gunjan…….you were the nicest.

And that time never arrived, and i was sure that it may never. I have not ever really gotten over Gunjan…and i really knew that you have bigger things in store for you….to me it seemed like you chose to score low….your potential was infinite….and I am a chamar boy ….getting Rs 20 a week, who just wanted to die.

And it got worse….skin gave away….and so did my belief of me being destined for self destruction.

I did misbehave with you and probably cheated you emotionally…..but you have to understand….you were the only good in my life back then.

I never really blamed for what happened next…i knew i deserved it….but i would have heard it from you.

It may be possible yet improbable that you might carry some guilt of what happened. Hope this helps….i deserved it and i am sorry.

I still miss you….for you are the only person who may still understand me.

For me, you are still the only person who is truly broken me…..it feels like you still have a part of me, and i still have yours. I still feel you…i still understand your emotions your hands your breath and those thousand different emotions your beautiful eyes conveyed.

I still imagine me exhaling on you soft skin and feeling your scent.
